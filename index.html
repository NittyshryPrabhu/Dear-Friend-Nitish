Dear Friend : Nitish

Wr : Nittyshry Prabhu



Nittyshry Prabhu , born on January 28, 2005, in Bhagalpur, Bihar, India, is a passionate writer, creative imagineer, and aspiring engineer with a unique blend of analytical thinking and imaginative storytelling. He balanced academic rigor(Enigineering Degree) with a deep love for literature and creative expression, continuously pushing the boundaries of their intellectual and creative pursuits.He completed his high school and secondary higher schooling from his village (Bhramarpur, Bhagalpur). After that he went to Delhi for graduation and did Bachelor of Computer Science [AI & ML] from MIT (Affiliated By Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow).

"Dear Friend : Nitish" is an autobiography written by Nittyshry Prabhu. It tells the story of friendship between Nitish and Nitty.


* Friendship is like a function of mathematics in our life, in which many sets are our friends. Our understanding is called relation. We are able to know each other, understand each other. And can do anything in life. Friend teaches everything that our parents sometimes cannot teach. Teaches us to stray a little, smile a little, love a little, and cross the limits. [ Nittyshry Prabhu ]
