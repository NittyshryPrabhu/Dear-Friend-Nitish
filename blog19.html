Scene 19

Going to Chandan sir's coaching on 1st day


I reached Nitish's house at 4:00 PM. Nitish calls everyone (Ankit, Adarsh, Sonal) over the phone.
After some time,
Sonal, Adarsh, Ankit reach Nitish's house. Everyone was riding their cycles. That day I did not have a cycle so I thought of going on Nitish's cycle because I had deliberately left my cycle at home that day so that I wanted to ride the cycle again with Nitish and enjoy that long journey/path.
I said to Nitish: Come, I will sit on your cycle.
Nitish: Yes, yes, sit.
Now all of us started the journey. It was fun obviously because going to a new place to study and that too with friends.
Chandan Sir's coaching was around 2 km away. We reached there dancing, singing and enjoying the journey.
When we reached there, only one or two students were sitting.
The most surprising scene was that there was only a stool to sit on, how will we study sitting on it.
After some time,
more students came and Chandan Sir also came.
Chandan Sir: Pandit ji, what is your name?
I am a little scared Nitish ......... Nitish Kumar!
Chandan Sir: Nitish, don't be afraid, I am not a lion, I am a teacher, I just teach.
In the same way, I asked the names and addresses of all my friends, we were also telling them a little nervously. Of course, it was the first day, nervousness is inevitable.
I said: Nitish, there is no greenery here.
Nitish: Greenery? There is a lot of greenery, come let me show you outside.
I said: Abe Yaar, not that greenery.
Nitish: Then which greenery?
I said: Well, you are not understanding which greenery I am talking about.
Nitish: Well, that greenery.
I said: Yes, that one.
Nitish: I don't know friend, it seems it is not here.
Ankit: Have you guys come to study or to see the greenery.
Sonal: I thought it is not at Sumit sir's place, but it is not here either.
Adarsh: Leave all that and look at the board, Newton's first law.
Chandan Sir: Yes, Pandit ji, do you all know Newton's first law? 10 seconds later, Chandan Sir, everyone knows this, today listen to me. Chandan Sir had a small whiteboard in his coaching and the place was also a little small, but his style of teaching was different from all others.
Studying physics is not an easy task. That day I saw how a teacher makes his students understand his point, i.e., a topic, and arouse the desire to understand it, to store it in their minds in a simple way.
I think Chandan Sir did not live in pretense.
On the first day, I saw that where we were going to study, there was only one fan installed. It was a simple looking concrete house.
All this was disturbing me a bit, but my friends and I found Chandan Sir's style of teaching very beautiful. So we decided that we will study Physics from Chandan Sir only.
