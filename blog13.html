Scene 13

Meeting with a new friend Krishna !

I said: Hey Krishna how are you?
Krishna: I am fine, and you?
I said: I am also fine bro.
Krishna: That brother of yours has not come.
I said: Who is my brother?
Krishna: The one who sits with you, both of you come together.
I said: Oh Nitish!
Krishna: Oh yes the same Nitish.
I said: Hey friends, he is not my brother, he is a good and true friend, Nitish.
Krishna: Oh I thought you both are brothers.
I said: But why did you think so?
Krishna: Hey brother, both of you have similar faces, and you sit together, walk together.
I said: Yes, it happens. He is my good and close friend only. Here Nitish has come.
Krishna: Hey Nitish I am Krishna.
I said: Nitish this is Krishna, our another new friend.
Nitish: Ok bro, we are friends.
Nitish: By the way Krishna, since when have you been studying here?
Krishna: Brother I have been studying here for almost 3 months, you guys have just come.
I said: How do you study?
Krishna: Studies are fine, you just have to study carefully.
Nitish: Yes yes .
I said: Ok friend, let's go to class, Gudar sir has come.
After some time!
Krishna :Tells me brother that beautiful girl comes with you everyday, who is she?
I said: Oh Shalu.
Krishna: I don't know her name.
Nitish:Oh Yaar, she is his neighbor. It's nothing like that brother.
Krishna: No, I just asked.
I said: Ok, it's okay,well do you have a girlfriend?
Krishna: Be a little shy brother, girlfriend.
Nitish: Yes, be shy, but I have just asked you, so you are getting feelings, right?
I said: Looks like he is a serious love-sick.
Krishna: No friend, nothing, but you both must be having a girlfriend.
Nitish: Yes yes why not, isn't it Bhabhi ji?
I said: Nitish you will get lost kid.
Krishna: Please tell me who is she? She comes here to study.
Nitish: No friend Bhabhi ji doesn't come here, she studies somewhere else.
I said: Nitish I will also tell you that you have not one but many girlfriends.
Krishna: Brother tell me their names.
I said: Nitish no please. Anyway she hasn't become Bhabhi yet.
Nitish: She will become one someday, right Krishna?
Krishna: Yes yes definitely.
I said: You crazy guy, you keep on saying anything.
