Scene 14

As time passed and everything changed, our friendship grew. We had to explain and understand each other because understanding everything without speaking shows the close bonding that we had between us.
Now we moved from 8th to class 9th and both of us grew one year older or we should say we became older but with new emotions and understanding.
Here we became friends with a good student friend Amlesh ji because he was also crazy like us and was honest and good too.
See, a person's thinking is based on his own control and mind. This is because when we met Amlesh ji, he used to talk to both of us by saying 'aap' (you) and we too, so maybe we used to call him Amlesh ji.
We made more new friends there like Sonal, Aditya with whom we used to have a lot of fun and study.
Both of us must have been around 15-16 years of age, full of enthusiasm, crazy and wild, always keeping the feeling of love and respect for everyone above everything.
When I was in class 9, I did not meet Riya but I always missed her, but I don't know what happened during that first meeting that she said Nitty give me your English book and I gave Nitish's book to Riya. I will always remember her style and simplicity because she was the first girl who made me different and the best. She taught me a lot without saying anything.
We had a lot of friends in class 9 and we both (Nitish and I) were different from all others, meaning we were always truthful and good.
