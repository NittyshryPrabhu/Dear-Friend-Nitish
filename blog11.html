1st day I'm going to Gudar sir's coaching!
I go to Shalu's house.
Her Nani says: Shalu is taking bath right now, Beta , sit for a while, she will be coming after getting ready. You will also go to the same place where Shalu goes to study.
I said: Yes grandmother.
Nani: Son, study well with full concentration, bring glory to your parents. Listen, go carefully on the way, take care of Shalu too.
I said: Yes Nani, don't take tension at all.
Shalu: Let's go now, how much do you talk? There is no need for you to take care of me.
I said: Why not, I need it a lot, after all you are my neighbor.
Shalu: Okay, let's tell your mother that I am your neighbor.
I said: Are you mad? I am joking.
Shalu: Yes, yes I am also joking, coward.
After some time when we reached the coaching!
I said: Hey, how many students are there here?
Shalu: Yes, a lot of students study here.
I said: I am feeling scared.
Shalu: It's okay, it's the first day, don't worry, I am here, okay.


I said: Yes.
I just went and stood with the boys. After some time, I saw that Nitish had also come there. I was shocked and felt very happy inside. I called out to Nitish.
Nitish: Hey, you are here.
I said: That's why you are also here.
