           Class 7
We did a lot of fun in class 7.
We had lots of fun and frolic like sometimes we played Kabaddi together, sometimes we played Bhagam Bhaag.

Among our friends, we two got the most love from the boys and Riya from the girls. Because we three (Nitish, Nitty, Riya) used to go to government school only on one day of the week and the other day we used to study in private school. We were a little average students in the private school but were considered the best in the government school.
In the government school:
Once the annual exam came in class 7. All the students had come, we both had also reached but Riya did not come that day. It so happened that my and Riya's writing was almost the same, so Nitish and Shalu, Khushi, Komal, all the friends started saying Nitty you write Riya's copy. That day I wrote Riya's exam copy and everyone started thinking that there is something between me and Riya. There was nothing but something started happening.
