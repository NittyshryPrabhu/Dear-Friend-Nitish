Dear Friend : Nitish

Wr : Nittyshry Prabhu

Scene 1

           Class 5
My( Nittyshry Prabhu / Nitty ) and Nitish's first meeting!
The teacher is teaching in the class and Nitish is drawing some pictures on my school dress.
After some time I come to know that he has drawn some pictures on my back with his pen and has also written my name.
When I came to know that he has written something on my school dress I got very angry and I told him that I will go and tell the teacher.
After this he also got a little scared and said please forgive me today, I don't know how I wrote my name on your dress, this won't happen please.
I was just about to go and tell this to his father. I don't know what happened between us, I didn't go to his father. That day we had a big fight. Nitish was very scared because he was very scared of his father and that day I left no stone unturned to scare him. I think that day was the foundation of our friendship, that too starting with a fight, what's the matter? After this our friendship deepened and we got to know each other very closely. We both used to sit together, used to fight with the teacher together, used to think alike.
